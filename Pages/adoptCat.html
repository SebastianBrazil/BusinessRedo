    Adoption Services

    Kitten Season is here and ACT (Abandoned Cat Team) have lots of cats and kittens available for adoption. All kittens and cats are spayed or neutered, tested (FIV / FeLV), vaccinated, dewormed, microchipped, and treated for fleas. Our adoption fee is $125 for kittens and cats. Most of our kittens are available to view at the Stockton Petsmart located at 10520 Trinity Parkway.
