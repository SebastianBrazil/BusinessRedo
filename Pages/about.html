    We are very proud of our clinic. It's a unique and special place staffed by animal lovers who care for our patients as if they were our own and are dedicated to saving lives through prevention of births. We know spaying & neutering works for everyone - especially the animals! ACT Spay/Neuter Clinic is operated by a 501(c)(3) non-profit animal charity, The Abandoned Cat Team of Stockton, California.

    Our clinic opened in late July of 2009 and is mentored by The National Spay Neuter Response Team from Humane Alliance of North Carolina. We are thrilled to have them as our partners. Donations from the public, a legacy from The Abandoned Cat Team's founder, Holly Pyle, and significant grant funding from PetSmart Charities of Phoenix, Arizona, as well as thousands of hours of work by volunteers who shared our mission brought our clinic into being and keep it running every day.
