    The many ways you can support ACT Spay/Neuter Clinic

    Volunteer

    Succeeding in our mission requires both clinic staff and volunteers. Distributing and posting fliers, tabling at local events, and helping our with fundraising are critical. An hour a day or a day a week you’re needed and appreciated. Don’t worry, we will teach you what you need to know.

    Donate

    Operating a high quality, low cost clinic is costly. Help by sending a tax deductible donation in memory of a special pet or person or just for you. We always need used towels, laundry supplies, canned cat and dog food, cat litter and newspaper.

    Planned Giving

    You can continue helping animals by remembering ACT in your will or easy monthly giving through United Way or your company's matching donation program.

    "Cans for Critters"

    Recycle your aluminum cans & plastic bottles to fund surgeries for those who cannot even afford our low prices and essential feral cat clinics. Drop your cans & bottles by the clinic.
