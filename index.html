    • Welcome to ACT Spay/Neuter Clinic

    San Joaquin County's Only Non-Profit Low Cost, High Quality, Spay/Neuter Clinic

    If you are looking for high quality, low cost spay or neuter surgery & basic vaccinations for your pets, as well as feral wild cats, you have reached the right place! Operated by the non-profit ‘The Abandoned Cat Team’, at ACT Spay/Neuter Clinic we are absolutely committed to our mission of ending the killing of over 15,000 homeless dogs, cats, kittens, puppies and all feral cats every year at San Joaquin shelters by preventing future births of unwanted litters.

    Spaying & neutering also makes your pets healthier and better behaved companions. ACT opened in Stockton in late 2009 and we have already safely and humanely sterilized thousands of animals. Let us put our very experienced & compassionate clinic team to work for you and the animals you care about.

    "There is no disease or condition of companion animals that takes more of their lives than euthanasia."

    - Janet M. Scarlet, DVM, MPH, PhD
